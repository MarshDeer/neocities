Welcome to my High‑Scores archive!

A couple years ago I built my own full-sized arcade cabinet, and every so often I get hit with the urge to try to improve my highscores in my favorite arcade games. Since my local arcades are terrible and don't have highscore persistence anyways, I decided to make this website to keep a public record of my (admittedly not amazing) highscores.

All these scores were achieved legitimately using a single credit and the ROM's default dip-switch settings.
